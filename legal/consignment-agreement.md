## 2. Revenue Split (70/30)
The Manager shall list the Vehicle across the éPure Drive platform and premium third-party marketplaces. 
Gross rental revenue generated directly by the Vehicle (excluding taxes, airport fees, or delivery fees) shall be distributed as follows:
- **70% to the Owner**
- **30% to the Manager** (éPure Drive LLC)
Payouts to the Owner will be processed on the [1st / 15th] of every month alongside a detailed performance ledger.

## 3. Maintenance, Tolls, and Wear & Tear
- **Preventative Maintenance**: The Owner is financially responsible for standard maintenance (oil changes, tire replacement, brakes). The Manager agrees to physically coordinate and transport the vehicle for such maintenance, deducting the costs transparently from the Owner’s next payout.
- **Tolls & Citations**: The Manager will assign a SunPass transponder to the vehicle. Any tolls or citations incurred during a rental period will be billed to the respective renter and hold no financial liability to the Owner.
- **Cleaning & Detailing**: The Manager will cover all costs associated with hand-washing and detailing the vehicle before and after every rental session.

## 4. Insurance & Liability
During any rental period brokered by the Manager, the Vehicle will be strictly protected by the Manager’s commercial rental policy or the third-party marketplace's primary collision/liability policy (e.g., Turo/Lula) up to $1,000,000. 
- The Owner is required to maintain a personal auto insurance policy indicating the vehicle is garaged in Florida.
- In the event of a covered loss during a rental, the Manager will process the claim and handle all repair logistics on behalf of the Owner.

## 5. Storage and Usage
When not rented, the Manager agrees to store the Vehicle at a secure location. The Manager will not use the Vehicle for personal transport beyond necessary refueling, washing, and delivery logistics.

## 6. Term and Termination
This agreement shall commence upon signature and remain in effect for a minimum duration of **[3/6/12] months**. Thereafter, either party may terminate this agreement with a 30-day written notice, allowing honoring of any preexisting rental bookings.
