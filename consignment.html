How It Works & The Rules

1

Vehicle Requirements

We accept premium luxury and exotic vehicles (e.g., Porsche, Audi, Mercedes, BMW, Ferrari). Vehicles must generally be year 2020 or newer, have a clean title (no rebuilt/salvage), and be in excellent mechanical and cosmetic condition.

2

Onboarding & Prep

Once approved, we bring the car in for a full inspection, install a discreet GPS tracker, and take professional photographs. We execute a legal Consignment Agreement protecting your asset.

3

Hands-Off Management

We handle everything: reservations, customer inquiries, background checks, airport deliveries, detailing, and logistics. You do nothing but collect payments.

4

Revenue Split

Profits are split transparently. Our standard split heavily favors the vehicle owner. You will receive a detailed monthly report outlining trips, mileage, and your total earnings.

5

Personal Use

Need your car for the weekend? No problem. Owners can block off dates in the calendar to use their own vehicle, as long as it isn't already booked by a client.
