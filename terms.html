1 Eligibility & Requirements

To rent a vehicle with éPure Drive, you must meet all of the following requirements at the time of rental:

  • Be at least 21 years of age (25+ required for vehicles valued over $75,000).
  • Hold a valid driver's license in good standing. Foreign licenses are accepted with an International Driving Permit (IDP).
  • Provide a valid credit or debit card in the renter's name for the security deposit.
  • Have valid personal auto insurance that covers rented vehicles, OR purchase our coverage at the time of rental.
  • Present a valid government-issued photo ID.
Additional drivers must also meet all eligibility requirements and be listed on the Rental Agreement prior to operating the vehicle. An additional driver fee of $50 per rental period may apply.

2 Reservations & Booking

All reservations are subject to vehicle availability. A reservation confirmation does not guarantee a specific vehicle but guarantees the vehicle class/category booked. éPure Drive reserves the right to substitute a vehicle of equal or greater value at no additional charge.

Reservations can be made via our website, WhatsApp, or email. A security deposit is required at the time of vehicle handoff. No payment is required to place a reservation.

By completing a reservation, you confirm that you have read and agree to these Terms & Conditions in their entirety.

4 Insurance & Coverage

éPure Drive vehicles are insured by the owner (Route 305 Rental Car). Renters are required to provide one of the following:

  1. Personal auto insurance (must be a USA-based policy that extends to rental vehicles). You are responsible for verifying that your personal policy covers rentals before picking up the vehicle.
  2. Credit card rental protection — coverage provided by your credit card issuer as secondary coverage. Contact your card issuer to confirm terms before relying on this option.
  3. éPure Drive Coverage — purchased at the time of rental. Options include:
    • Standard Coverage: Included with certain vehicles. Deductible: $1,000 for vehicles under $35,000; $2,500 for vehicles $35,000 and above.
    • Premium Coverage: $149/day. Reduces your deductible to $0.
No Insurance / Declined Coverage: If you decline to provide valid insurance and decline to purchase our coverage, you assume full and unlimited personal liability for any damage to the vehicle, injuries to third parties, and property damage — regardless of fault.

Coverage is automatically voided if any prohibited use occurs (see Section 5), if you fail to report an accident promptly, or if you provide false information in connection with a claim.

5 Vehicle Use & Prohibited Uses

The Vehicle must be operated only by Authorized Drivers listed on the Rental Agreement. The following uses are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate termination of the rental, forfeiture of the security deposit, and void of all insurance coverage:

  • Operation by anyone not listed as an Authorized Driver.
  • Operating under the influence of alcohol, prescription drugs, or controlled substances.
  • Use for any illegal purpose or in violation of any law.
  • Use for hire (rideshare, taxi, delivery, etc.) or for pushing or towing any vehicle or object.
  • Participation in any race, speed test, or contest.
  • Teaching anyone to drive.
  • Transporting hazardous, flammable, or illegal materials.
  • Operation outside the State of Florida without prior written approval.
  • Driving on unpaved, off-road, or flooded roads.
  • Carrying more passengers than the Vehicle has seat belts.
  • Transporting children without legally required and properly installed child safety seats.
  • Odometer tampering or disconnection.
  • Continued operation when fluid levels (oil, coolant, etc.) are dangerously low.
  • Smoking, vaping, or using e-cigarettes inside the Vehicle.
  • Transporting animals without prior written approval.

6 Damage, Loss & Liability

You are responsible for all damage to the Vehicle during the rental period, regardless of fault or cause, including acts of nature, weather, terrain, and vandalism.

In the event of an accident or damage:

  1. Stop immediately and ensure the safety of all parties.
  2. Call 911 if there are injuries or significant property damage.
  3. Contact éPure Drive immediately via WhatsApp at (786) 209-6770.
  4. Do not admit fault or make any statements to other parties regarding liability.
  5. Document the scene with photos and obtain witness information.
  6. File a police report if required by law.

7 Fuel Policy

Vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and must be returned with a full tank. If the Vehicle is returned with less fuel than it was delivered with, a refueling fee of $15 per gallon (or current market rate, whichever is higher) plus a service fee of $35 will be charged.

If you purchase the Prepaid Fuel Option ($95), you may return the Vehicle at any fuel level. No refund is issued for unused fuel under this option.

8 Mileage Policy

Each rental includes a mileage allowance as stated in the Rental Agreement. Mileage is tracked via the odometer reading at pickup and return. Any miles driven beyond the included allowance will be billed at $0.25 per mile (or as otherwise specified in the Rental Agreement) and charged at the time of return.

If the odometer is found to have been tampered with or disconnected, mileage charges will be estimated based on route and usage history, and you will be liable for the maximum estimated overage plus a $500 tampering fee.

9 Cancellation & Modifications

éPure Drive offers free cancellation for reservations cancelled at least 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made within 48 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee equal to one day's rental rate.

To cancel or modify a reservation, contact us via WhatsApp at (786) 209-6770 or email route305.rental@gmail.com.

Rental period extensions are subject to vehicle availability and must be requested and confirmed in writing before the original due-in date. The extended period will be billed at the current daily rate.

Early returns: If you return the vehicle before the agreed due-in date, no refund will be issued for unused rental days unless otherwise agreed in writing at the time of booking.

10 Tolls & Traffic Violations

You are solely responsible for all tolls, parking citations, red-light camera fees, speed camera fees, and any other fines or violations incurred during your rental period.

Florida operates a cashless toll system (SunPass) on many roads. If you drive on toll roads, you must use a personal SunPass transponder or pay via the Florida Toll-by-Plate system. Unpaid tolls may result in a fine plus an administrative fee of $25 per toll transaction.

If we receive a violation notice related to your rental, you authorize us to charge the full amount of the fine plus a $35 administrative processing fee to your payment method on file.

11 Personal Data & Privacy

We collect personal information (name, driver's license, contact details, payment information) necessary to process your rental. This information is used solely for rental operations and may be disclosed to law enforcement when required by law.

Vehicles may be equipped with GPS tracking and event data recorders. By renting the Vehicle, you consent to monitoring of the vehicle's location and operational data. This data may be used to enforce the terms of this Agreement.

Before returning the Vehicle, please delete all personal data from the vehicle's infotainment system (paired phones, navigation history, contacts).
