Here's how the platform works for you and your customers.

🌐 Your Public Fleet Page
—
Share this with your customers to browse your cars.
👤 Operator Workflow
1 Add your cars — Go to Fleet tab, click "Add Car" and fill in the details.
2 Share your public page — Copy the link above and send it to customers.
3 Manage bookings — Confirm, track, and complete rentals from the Bookings tab.
4 Invite your team — Add staff or finance members from Staff & Users (Pro).
🧑 Customer Workflow
1 Browse your fleet — Customer visits your public link and sees your available cars.
2 Select a car — They pick dates, pickup location, and add-ons.
3 Book & pay — Checkout via Stripe; booking is confirmed automatically.
4 Sign rental agreement — Customer receives and signs the agreement before pickup.
