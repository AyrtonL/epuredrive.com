Coexistence during Phase 1

During Phase 1, the new Next.js app handles epuredrive.com (marketing + signup) and {slug}.epuredrive.com (tenant fleet). The existing admin/dashboard.html stays live at epuredrive.com/admin/dashboard.html during the transition — tenants get linked to it from the new dashboard entry page until Phase 2 completes the migration.
