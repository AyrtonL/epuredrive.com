What "example right away" means in practice

When a tenant first logs in, the dashboard's Fleet Customizer shows a pre-populated demo of what their public fleet page will look like — with placeholder cars and their company name already applied. They can see it, customize it, and hit "Publish" to make it live at {their-slug}.epuredrive.com. No guesswork about what it'll look like before they go live.
